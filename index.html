THE FIRST K-MUSIC NFT MARKET IN THE WORLD!

Inspired by Soundcloud, we are building a Web 3.0 based decentralized music streaming platform.
Bibimbeat is the music market where artists can mint music NFTs,
and K-Music Fans can listen to their NFTs, buy and sell their favorite artists' NFT.

We are accepting 100 testers for beta test.
And testers can participate in Bibimbeat's Initial NFT Offering(INO)!
